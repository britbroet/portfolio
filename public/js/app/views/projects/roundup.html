RoundUp

View demo video

WHAT

RoundUp is an app that allows interviewers to track a candidate through an interview round. Users sign in and can add notes about questions asked of a candidate during the interview they participated in. This allows other interviewers to keep track of what questions have already been asked of a candidate, and to see other interviewers notes so far. Ultimately, with all interviewers' notes in one central location, the interview round page can serve as a great way to review the round.

WHO

This was a team project with three collaborators including myself.

HOW

RoundUp was built using NodeJS, Javascript, jQuery, AngularJS, and MongoDB.
